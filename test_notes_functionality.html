🎯 How to Use

  1. Click "Add Note" to create a new note
  2. Fill in title and content, then click "Save Note"
  3. Use the thumbtack icon to pin important notes
  4. Click edit to modify existing notes
  5. Click delete to remove notes
  6. Pinned notes automatically sort to the top
